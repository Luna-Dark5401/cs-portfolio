To draw usually you need the following:
    Pencil, Eraser, Paper, Black Pen(for outline if wanted), Color Pencils or Markers(optional), Music(optional).

How to draw

Eyes Nose Hair/Mouth

Hair

  1. First the hair, now the doing hair is quite simple.If you have the outline of the head you want to start making and outline of the hair you wish to have. If not then you ceate a circle and then have to lines parrallel to the ends of the circle.After that connect the two lines into a triangle with curve edges so it looks like cheeks.
  2. Next after the hair is outlined how you want it, you add the many strands of it and make sure to add some irregular stands so it could look somewhat realistic

Mouth

  1. First we can create a line to make the part between the upper and lower lips.
  2. Next is all up to you if you want big lips but the basic thing to so is to create small half bubbles that look like lips and the lower lip would be bigger than the upper lip usually.
