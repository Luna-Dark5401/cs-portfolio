To draw usually you need the following:
    Pencil, Eraser, Paper, Black Pen(for outline if wanted), Color Pencils or Markers(optional), Music(optional).

Eyes


Steps:

  1. First create an almond like shape for one of the eyes at least, right or left your choice, this will be the outline of the eye.
  2. Next creat a circle inside the almond shaped eye and make sure that it isn't close to the lower part of the shape give it some space.
  3. Now every eye has a pupil don't they,so create a smaller circle inside the circle from before.
  4. You now have the basic form of an eye and the next step to that is to create the lashes even if its a boy.Create gentle straigh curvy lines to make the lashes look a bit realistic.
  5. Don't forget the eyebrows, now its up to you if you want to have realistic eyebrows or just a line eyebrow.
